# TokenGate Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the TokenGate project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior includes:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project
- Showing empathy towards others

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Use of sexualized language or imagery
- Other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to solidworkssa@gmail.com. All reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly and fairly.
